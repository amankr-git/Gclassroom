Fee structure

    Fee Structure:

  • The course will be conducted on self-finance basis.
  • SC/ST candidates will be allowed 20% concession in tuition fee.
  • Besides the above noted fees and charges, each candidate appearing in the University examination will be required to pay the prescribed examination fee to the University for each examination.
  • The fee may be revised by the University in changing circumstances.
  • Preference in admission will be given to those passing out from Madrasa System of Education.
